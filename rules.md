# Aloe Toons Content Rules

## Compliance for Forever Living Supplements

All content produced in this project (scripts, prompts, voice-over, generated videos) must follow these rules.

### Never Make Health Claims

- No "cures," "treats," "heals," or medical advice
- No diagnoses or condition-specific claims
- No claims about preventing or treating disease

### Never Make Weight Loss Claims

- No "lose X kg," "guaranteed results," or specific outcome promises
- No extreme before/after promises
- No claims about body composition changes

### Allowed Language

Focus on:

- Routine, consistency, habits, structure
- General wellness and lifestyle
- "Feel," "support," "maintain," "routine"
- Humor and personality (as in mascot scripts)

### Scope

These rules apply to:

- All scripts in `scripts.csv`
- All Replicate prompts in `prompts.py`
- Voice-over text and TTS output
- Any generated or edited content for reels
